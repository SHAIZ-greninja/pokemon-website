POKEMON BEGINING WITH C

CACNEA

TYPE: Grass

Cacnea is a cactus-inspired Pokémon that lives in arid environments. It uses the water it stores in its body to withstand long periods of drought. It attacks by shooting thorns from its body. Cacnea evolves into Cacturne starting at level 32

Abilities: Sand Veil (Increases evasion in a sandstorm) Water Absorb (Hidden Ability: Heals 25% of maximum HP when hit by a Water-type move)

Moves: Absorb, Needle Arm, Growth, Leech Seed, Sand Attack, Pin Missile, Spikes, Energy Ball, Payback, Destiny Bond

CACTURNE

TYPE: Grass/Dark

When the sun goes down, Cacturne becomes active. It lures desert travelers, waiting for the right moment to strike. This Pokémon is known for its patience in hunting.

Abilities: Sand Veil Water Absorb (Hidden Ability)

Moves: Faint Attack, Needle Arm, Sucker Punch, Spikes, Dark Pulse, Solar Beam, Sandstorm, Hyper Beam

CALYREX

TYPE: Psychic/Grass

LEGENDARY POKEMON

Calyrex is a legendary Pokémon from the Crown Tundra. It's revered as a king that once ruled over Galar's frozen landscapes. It has an air of mystery and is believed to have immense wisdom. Calyrex has two forms when fused with either Glastrier (Ice-type) or Spectrier (Ghost-type).

Abilities: Unnerve (Prevents opposing Pokémon from eating held Berries)

Moves: *Glacial Lance, Astral Barrage, Psyshock, Giga Drain, Psychic, Energy Ball, Leech Seed, *Calm Mind, Agility

CAMERUPT

TYPE: Fire/Ground

Camerupt is a Fire/Ground-type Pokémon resembling a volcanic camel. The humps on its back erupt with magma when it gets angry. It's slow but packs immense power in its Fire-type moves.

Abilities: Magma Armor (Prevents freezing) Solid Rock (Reduces damage from super-effective moves) Anger Point (Hidden Ability: Maxes Attack stat when hit by a critical hit)

Moves: Eruption, Lava Plume, Earthquake, Rock Slide, Overheat, Flamethrower, Yawn

DOES NOT EVOLVE

CARBINK

TYPE: Rock/Fairy

Carbink is a Rock/Fairy-type Pokémon formed in caves under extreme pressure. It is adorned with precious gems and has high defensive stats, making it a sturdy wall in battles. It's also known as the servant to the legendary Pokémon Diancie.

Abilities: Clear Body (Prevents stat reduction by opponents' moves or abilities) Sturdy (Prevents one-hit KO moves)

Moves: Diamond Storm, Moonblast, Reflect, Light Screen, Power Gem, Stealth Rock, Explosion, Toxic

DOES NOT EVOLVE
