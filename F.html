POKEMON BEGINING WITH F

FALINKS

TYPE: Fighting

Falinks consists of a group of six small Pokémon that move as a unit. It has a unique marching formation with the leader at the front and five soldiers following behind. They are disciplined fighters that train together.

Abilities: Battle Armor: Prevents the Pokémon from receiving critical hits. Hidden Ability: Defiant: When any of the Pokémon’s stats are lowered, its Attack stat increases by two stages.

Moves: No Retreat, Close Combat, First Impression, Brick Break, Iron Defense

FARFETCHD

TYPE: Fighting/ Normal / Flying

Farfetch’d is a bird that always carries a leek with it, using it both for food and as a weapon. The Galarian variant is more focused on combat and is known for its pride.

Abilities: Keen Eye: Prevents accuracy reduction. Inner Focus: Prevents flinching. Hidden Ability: Scrappy (Normal Farfetch’d): Allows the user to hit Ghost-types with Normal and Fighting-type moves.

Moves: Leaf Blade, Brave Bird, Knock Off, Fury Cutter, Swords Dance

FEAROW

TYPE: Normal / Flying

Fearow is a large bird Pokémon with an elongated beak designed for pecking and spearing prey. It’s known for its impressive flight endurance, able to travel long distances without tiring.

Abilities: Keen Eye: Prevents accuracy reduction. Hidden Ability: Sniper: Powers up critical-hit damage.

Moves: Drill Peck, Agility, Drill Run, Sky Attack, Roost

FEEBAS

TYPE: Water

Feebas is considered one of the ugliest Pokémon, but its unappealing looks hide great potential. Though weak in battle, it can evolve into the elegant Milotic, which is highly sought after.

Abilities: Swift Swim: Doubles the user’s Speed in rain. Oblivious: Prevents the Pokémon from becoming infatuated or taunted. Hidden Ability: Adaptability: Increases the power of moves that share the same type as the user.

Moves: Tackle, Splash, Flail, Water Pulse

FENNEKIN

TYPE: Fire

Fennekin is a small fox-like Pokémon that uses its fur to store heat. It enjoys eating twigs, which it uses to fuel its fire-based attacks. It’s a very loyal Pokémon, but it can be temperamental.

Abilities: Blaze: Powers up Fire-type moves when the Pokémon’s HP is low. Hidden Ability: Magician: Steals the opponent’s held item when the Pokémon uses a move.

Moves: Ember, Psybeam, Fire Spin, Flamethrower, Howl
