POKEMON BEGINING WITH B

BAGON

TYPE: Dragon

Bagon is a 'Dragon-type'. It is known as the Rock Head Pokémon and is part of a three-stage evolutionary line. Bagon evolves into **Shelgon** at level 30 and then into 'Salamence' at level 50. Here's more detail about Bagon:

Abilities: Rock Head (Primary Ability): Prevents recoil damage from moves like Double-Edge. Sheer Force (Hidden Ability): Boosts the power of moves that have secondary effects, but prevents those effects.

Rage, Ember, Bite, Dragon Breath, Zen Headbutt, Headbutt, Dragon Claw, Flamethrower, Shadow Claw, Crunch, Rock Slide, Dragon Dance, Dragon Rush, Twister

BALTOY

TYPE: Ground/Psychic

Baltoy is a dual-type Ground/PsychiC. It is based on ancient clay figurines and has a mystical, enigmatic design. Baltoy is known for spinning on one leg like a top, and in battles, it uses a mix of offensive and defensive Psychic- and Ground-type moves. Its Psychic nature gives it access to moves that inflict confusion or control the battlefield, while its Ground typing provides defensive strength against Electric-type moves.

Abilities: Levitate (immunity to Ground-type moves) Evolution: Baltoy evolves into 'Claydol' starting at level 36.

Confusion, Rapid Spin, Mud-Slap, Psybeam, Ancient Power, Extrasensory, Earth Power, Cosmic Power, Psychic, Earthquake, Shadow Ball, Calm Mind

BANETTE

TYPE: Ghost

Banette is a 'Ghost-type' Pokémon, known for its creepy and eerie appearance. It is often depicted as a plush doll that has come to life, filled with hatred and seeking revenge on the person who abandoned it. Here are some details about Banette. Banette's moveset is versatile, allowing it to inflict status ailments and deal significant damage with physical or special attacks. Its Mega Evolution boosts its Attack and allows it to become a more formidable offensive presence in battle.

Ability: Insomnia (prevents sleep) Frisk (reveals opponent's held item) Cursed Body (Hidden Ability - has a chance to disable a move that hits it)

Moves: Shadow Sneak, Hex, Curse, Will-O-Wisp, Sucker Punch, Shadow Ball, Thunderbolt, Psychic, Shadow Claw, Giga Impact, Knock Off, Destiny Bond, Imprison

BARBARACLE

TYPE: Rock / Water

Barbaracle is a dual-type Pokémon, being Rock and Water. It evolves from Clawitzer and is known for its unique appearance resembling a combination of a barnacle and a bipedal creature. Barbaracle is known for its decent Attack and Speed stats, making it effective in offensive roles. Its unique typing gives it some interesting resistances and weaknesses in battles.

Abilities: Pickpocket: This ability allows Barbaracle to steal an item from a Pokémon that hits it with a move.

Moves: Surf, Stone Edge, Rock Slide, Liquidation

BARBOACH

TYPE: Water/Ground

Barboach is a Water/Ground-type Pokémon, introduced in Generation III. It resembles a small, catfish-like creature. Barboach's dual typing allows it to resist Electric-type moves, while it can utilize moves like Earthquake for STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus) damage. It often serves as a versatile Pokémon in battles due to its unique typing.

Abilities: Oblivious: Prevents it from being infatuated or taunted. Anticipation: Alerts the Pokémon if the opponent has a super-effective move.

Moves: Water Gun, Mud-Slap, Bite, Whirlpool, Amnesia, Mud Bomb, Fissure, Surf, Earthquake, ce Beam, Scald, Rock Slide, Aqua Ring, Waterfall, Yawn
