POKEMON BEGINING WITH A

ABOMASNOW

TYPE:: Grass/Ice

Abomasnow is a dual-type Grass/Ice Pokémon. It evolves from 'Snover' starting at level 40. Abomasnow is also known as the "Frost Tree Pokémon" and has a Mega Evolution, Mega Abomasnow, which increases its power significantly.

Abilities: Snow Warning: Causes hail to begin falling when Abomasnow enters the battle (and Mega Abomasnow has this ability as well). Mega Abomasnow: When it Mega Evolves, its Attack, Special Attack, and Defense stats are significantly boosted. However, its Speed is lowered. Mega Abomasnow is bulkier and hits harder, making it an even more dangerous foe during hailstorms.

Strengths and Weaknesses: Strong Against: Water, Ground, Rock (due to its Grass typing), and Flying, Dragon, Grass (due to its Ice typing). Weak Against: Fire, Bug, Poison, Fighting, Flying, Rock, Steel, and other Ice-types (due to its combination of Grass and Ice typing).

Moves: Ice Punch, Wood Hammer, Blizzard, Razor Leaf, Aurora Veil, Earthquake, Solar Beam, Focus Blast, Energy Ball, Leech Seed, Stomp,

ABRA

TYPE: Psychic

Abra is a 'Psychic-type' Pokémon from the first generation (Gen I) of the Pokémon series. Known as the "Psi Pokémon," Abra is notable for its strong psychic powers and ability to teleport. Abra is known for sleeping for most of the day, up to 18 hours. It uses its psychic powers even in its sleep, allowing it to sense danger and teleport to a safe location if needed. Abra’s main form of defense is **teleportation**. It can sense the presence of others and use its psychic abilities to warp to a different location.

Abilities: Synchronize: Passes on a burn, poison, or paralysis to the foe if Abra becomes affected by it. Inner Focus: Prevents flinching. Hidden Ability Magic Guard: Prevents indirect damage, like from status effects or weather conditions.

Moves: Psybeam,Confusion, Psyshock, Future Sight, Shadow Ball, Energy Ball

ABSOL

TYPE: Dark

Absol is a 'Dark-type Pokémon' Known as the "Disaster Pokémon," Absol is highly attuned to natural disasters and is often believed to bring them, though in reality, it warns people of impending dangers. Absol is often portrayed as a solitary and misunderstood Pokémon. It lives in rugged mountains and rarely interacts with humans unless it senses a catastrophe. Due to its association with disasters, many people mistakenly blame Absol for causing misfortunes, though it is merely a messenger.

Abilities: Pressure (standard ability): This ability causes the opponent to use more PP when attacking Absol. Super Luck(Hidden Ability): This increases the critical-hit ratio of Absol's moves. Justified (Mega Absol's ability): Increases Attack if hit by a Dark-type move.

Moves: Sucker Punch, Night Slash, Psycho Cut, Slash, Shadow Claw, Thunder Wave, Ice Beam, Flamethrower, Megahorn, Perish Song, Swords Dance, Knock Off, Play Rough

ACCELGOR

TYPE: Bug

Accelgor is a 'Bug-type; Pokémon. It evolves from **Shelmet** when traded with another player who is trading a **Karrablast**, which simultaneously evolves into 'Escavalier'. Accelgor is known for its incredible speed and nimbleness. It loses its heavy shell upon evolving, which makes it one of the fastest Bug-type Pokémon in existence. Its design is based on a ninja, which explains its speed and appearance.

Abilities: Hydration (Heals status conditions if it's raining) Sticky Hold (Prevents the Pokémon's held item from being stolen or knocked off) Unburden (Hidden Ability, doubles Speed if the Pokémon loses its held item)

Moves: Acid Spray, Double Team, Water Shuriken, Absorb, Quick Attack, Mega Drain, Swift Me First, Agility, Struggle Bug, Giga Drain, U-turn, Bug Buzz, Recover, Guard Swap, Final Gambit, Toxic, Encore, Toxic, Hyper Beam, Giga Impact, Magical Leaf, Facade, Protect,Light Screen, Reflect, Safeguard, Rest, Thief, Snore, Protect, Swift, Sunny Day, Facade, Payback,Round, Leech Life, Encore, Spikes, Guard Swap, Power Swap, Feint, Baton Pass, Acid Armor,Bug Bite, Signal Beam, Giga Drain, Electroweb

AIPOM

TYPE: Normal

Aipom is a Normal-type Pokémon. It is known for its playful and mischievous nature, often using its tail like a hand to grasp objects or hang from tree branches. Aipom resembles a small, purple monkey with a large hand at the end of its tail, which it uses for most of its activities, like climbing and interacting with its environment.

Abilities: Run Away: Ensures escape from wild Pokémon battles. Pickup: Allows Aipom to pick up items after battle. Hidden Ability (Dream World): Skill Link (increases the likelihood of multi-strike moves hitting the maximum number of times).

Moves: Scratch, Astonish, Fury Swipes, Double Hit, Swift, Agility, Nasty Plot, Return, Shadow Claw, Thunderbolt, Aerial Ace, Bounce, Fake Out
