POKEMON BEGINING WITH G

GABITE

TYPE: Dragon/Ground

Gabite is known to be incredibly territorial and hoards shiny objects like treasures. It digs tunnels in caves and deserts.

Abilities: Sand Veil: Raises the Pokémon's evasiveness in a sandstorm. Rough Skin (Hidden Ability): Inflicts damage to attackers using direct contact moves.

Moves: Dragon Claw, Earthquake, Sandstorm, Dual Chop

GALLADE

TYPE: Psychic/Fighting

Gallade is a knight-like Pokémon known for its chivalry and ability to sense its opponent’s thoughts and intentions. It uses its elbow blades to fight skillfully.

Abilities: Steadfast: Raises Speed when flinched. Justified (Hidden Ability): Raises the Pokémon’s Attack stat when hit by a Dark-type move.

Moves: Psycho Cut, Close Combat, Close Combat, Swords Dance

GALVANTULA

TYPE: Bug/Electric

Galvantula spins electrically charged threads to ensnare prey. Its electric webs can paralyze opponents and it often uses this to its advantage in battle.

Abilities: Compound Eyes: Increases accuracy by 30%. Unnerve (Hidden Ability): Prevents opposing Pokémon from eating held Berries. Description: Galvantula spins electrically charged threads to ensnare prey. Its electric webs can paralyze opponents and it often uses this to its advantage in battle.

Moves: Thunder. Bug Buzz, Sticky Web, Electroweb

GARBODOR

TYPE: Poison

Garbodor is the embodiment of pollution, resembling a pile of toxic waste. It breaks down garbage and releases poison as a defense mechanism.

Abilities: Stench: May cause the target to flinch. Weak Armor: Lowers Defense when hit but raises Speed. Aftermath (Hidden Ability): Damages the opponent if Garbodor faints by physical contact.

Moves: Gunk Shot, Toxic, Sludge Bomb, Explosion

GARCHOMP

TYPE: Dragon/Ground

Garchomp is a fast and powerful dragon-like Pokémon that resembles a jet plane. It moves at high speeds in the air, and its sharp claws and incredible strength make it a dangerous opponent.

Abilities: Sand Veil: Raises evasiveness during a sandstorm. Rough Skin (Hidden Ability): Deals damage to opponents using direct contact moves.

Moves: Dragon Rush, Earthquake, Swords Dance, Stone Edge
