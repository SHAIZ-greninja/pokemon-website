POKEMON BEGINING WITH D

DARKRAI

TYPE: Dark

MYTHICAL POKEMON

Darkrai is a Mythical Dark-type Pokémon known for its ability to induce nightmares. It appears on moonless nights, and its presence is said to cause endless nightmares to those nearby. Despite its eerie reputation, it is not malicious by nature.

Abilities: Bad Dreams (Causes sleeping opposing Pokémon to lose HP at the end of each turn)

Moves: Dark Void, Dark Pulse, Nightmare, Hypnosis, Dream Eater, Nasty Plot, Shadow Ball, Psychic, Sludge Bomb, Thunderbolt

DARMANITAN

TYPE: Fire/Psychic

Darmanitan is known for its fiery temperament and sheer strength. In its **Standard Mode**, it focuses on physical attacks, while in **Zen Mode**, it becomes more defensive and gains Psychic abilities. Its Zen Mode reflects calmness and increased mental focus.

Abilities: Sheer Force (Increases the power of moves with additional effects but removes those effects) Zen Mode (Switches to Zen Mode below 50% HP, gaining Psychic typing and becoming more defensive)

Moves: Flare Blitz, Fire Punch, Superpower, Earthquake, Psychic, Zen Headbutt, Belly Drum, Stone Edge, Hammer Arm, U-turn

DARTRIX

TYPE: Grass/Flying

Dartrix is the evolved form of Rowlet and is known for its stylish appearance and precision when attacking with feather blades. Despite its elegance, it’s prone to becoming vain and distracted by its appearance.

Abilities: Overgrow (Boosts the power of Grass-type moves when HP is low) Long Reach (Hidden Ability: Allows the Pokémon to use contact moves without making physical contact)

Moves: Razor Leaf, Leaf Blade, Peck, Sucker Punch, Pluck, Ominous Wind, Roost, U-turn, Synthesis, Brave Bird

DARUMAKA

TYPE: Fire (Standard Form), Ice (Galarian Form)

Darumaka is the pre-evolved form of Darmanitan. In its **Standard Form**, it is a fiery, energetic Pokémon that loves hot climates, while in its **Galarian Form**, it is an Ice-type adapted to colder environments. Darumaka is full of energy, and its internal flame goes out when it sleeps.

Abilities: Hustle (Boosts Attack but lowers accuracy) Inner Focus (Hidden Ability for Galarian Form: Prevents flinching)

Moves: Fire Fang, Ice Punch, Flame Wheel, Tackle, Incinerate, Work Up, Headbutt, Bite, Avalanche, Belly Drum

DECIDUEYE

TYPE: Grass/Ghost

Abilities: Overgrow (Boosts the power of Grass-type moves when HP is low) Long Reach (Hidden Ability: Allows the Pokémon to use contact moves without making physical contact)

Moves: Spirit Shackle, Leaf Blade, Sucker Punch, Brave Bird, Shadow Sneak, Phantom Force, Roost, Leaf Storm, Nasty Plot, U-turn
