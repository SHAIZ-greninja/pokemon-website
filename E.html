POKEMON BEGINING WITH E

EELEKTRIK

TYPE: Electric

Eelektrik is the middle evolution of Tynamo. It looks like a small eel-like creature. Eelektrik evolves into **Eelektross** when exposed to a Thunder Stone. It is known for its Levitate ability, which gives it immunity to Ground-type moves, usually a big weakness for Electric-type Pokémon.

Abilities: Levitate: Makes the Pokémon immune to Ground-type moves, nullifying one of its main weaknesses.

Moves: Thunderbolt, Crunch, Discharge, Thunder Wave, Acid Spray, Zap Cannon

EELEKTROSS

TYPE: Electric

Eelektross is the final evolution of Tynamo, resembling a large eel with arms. It doesn’t have any weaknesses because of its Levitate ability, which makes it immune to Ground-type attacks, a typical weakness for Electric-types.

Abilities: Levitate: Renders the Pokémon immune to Ground-type moves, eliminating its only natural weakness.

Moves: Wild Charge, Flamethrower, Thunder Punch, Dragon Claw, Giga Drain, Volt Switch, Aqua Tail

EEVEE

TYPE: Normal

Eevee is known for its potential to evolve into many different forms depending on the method of evolution used. These include Flareon (Fire), Vaporeon (Water), Jolteon (Electric), and others.

Abilities: Run Away: Guarantees escape from wild Pokémon battles. Adaptability: Increases the STAB (Same-Type Attack Bonus) from 1.5x to 2x, making Eevee’s Normal-type moves stronger. Anticipation (Hidden Ability): The Pokémon shudders if the opponent has a move that is super-effective against it.

Moves: Quick Attack, Bite, Swift, Baby-Doll Eyes, Charm, Tackle, Take Down

EISCUE

TYPE: Ice

`

Eiscue is a penguin-like Pokémon with a unique ability that changes its form. It has two forms: 'Ice Face' and 'Noice Face'. When in Ice Face form, Eiscue can absorb a physical attack without taking damage.

Abilities: Ice Face: When Eiscue is in its Ice Face form, the first physical attack will not deal any damage, and the Ice Face will be destroyed, changing Eiscue into its Noice Face form. It can return to Ice Face form if the weather becomes hail.

Moves: Icicle Crash, Freeze-Dry, Hail, Amnesia, Icy Wind, Aqua Jet, Headbutt, Surf

EKANS

TYPE: Poison

Ekans is a snake-like Pokémon. It evolves into **Arbok** starting at level 22. Ekans is a stealthy predator that wraps around its prey to immobilize them.

Abilities: Intimidate: Lowers the opponent’s Attack stat when Ekans enters battle. Shed Skin: Has a 1-in-3 chance of curing Ekans from status conditions at the end of each turn. Unnerve (Hidden Ability): Prevents the opponent from using berries.

Moves: Notable Moves, Poison Sting, Bite, Wrap, Glare, Acid, Crunch, Gunk Shot
